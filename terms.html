Enrollment & Minor Provisions

Our center welcomes learners of all ages. To ensure a legal and safe environment, specific registration requirements apply:

Adult Learners (18+)

Adults may register independently and are solely responsible for their conduct and adherence to the Center's professional standards during all sessions.

Young Learners (Under 18)

Enrollment must be completed by a legal guardian. Guardians agree to monitor their child's digital interactions and maintain communication with Center faculty.

Sessions & Scheduling

Efficiency is key to language acquisition. We maintain a strict scheduling policy to respect the time of both students and instructors:

24-Hour Cancellation Rule

Sessions must be rescheduled or cancelled at least 24 hours in advance. Failure to do so results in a full session charge to compensate the instructor's time.

Punctuality Policy

Instructors will wait for 15 minutes. If a student is absent beyond this window without notice, the session is forfeited and considered complete.

Student Safeguarding & Conduct

We provide a sanctuary for learning. Conduct that compromises the psychological or physical safety of our members is strictly prohibited:

  • visibility Professional Environment: All sessions must occur in a quiet, appropriate setting. Proper attire is required for all video interactions regardless of age.
  • forum Communication: Interactions between students and tutors must remain within Center-approved platforms. Private contact for minors is strictly forbidden.
  • block Zero Tolerance: Harassment, discriminatory language, or inappropriate content sharing results in immediate, permanent expulsion without refund.

Academic Integrity & Materials

Our curriculum is the result of years of expert pedagogical research. Use of these materials is governed by specific intellectual property rights:

Personal Use License

Materials are provided solely for the enrolled student's personal development. Recording sessions or distributing PDF worksheets is a violation of international copyright.

Homework & Testing

Students are expected to submit their own work. Reliance on unauthorized AI tools or external translation services during assessments undermines the learning process and may result in a non-completion status.

Financial Terms & Refunds

Transparency in billing ensures a healthy partnership between the Center and our students:

  • • Prepayment is required for all session blocks and group courses.
  • • Refunds are eligible only if requested within 14 days of purchase and before the start of the first session.
  • • Unused session credits expire 180 days after the date of purchase.

Self-Paced Modules

Our self-paced learning option gives you the flexibility to study on your own schedule while maintaining access to expert tutor support:

  • • Each course consists of 2 modules, each lasting 1 month. Modules may be purchased individually or together as a full course.
  • • The full self-paced course spans 3 months in total, covering both modules plus a consolidation period.
  • • Every self-paced enrolment includes 2 hours of online tutor consultation, divided into 4 sessions of 30 minutes each, to be scheduled at your convenience.
  • • Tutor consultation sessions must be used within the active module period and do not carry over to subsequent modules.
  • • Access to module materials is provided through the LinguaBridge e-Portal for the duration of the purchased module(s).
  • • Refunds for self-paced modules follow the same policy as live sessions: eligible within 14 days of purchase and before accessing course materials.
